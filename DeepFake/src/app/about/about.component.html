Deepfakes, You, and Us

Have you ever heard the term "Deepfake" before? In recent years, Deepfake has become a hot topic for a lot of people. Despite this, Deepfakes are still a mystery to a lot of people. So what are Deepfakes exactly? Simply put, Deepfakes are media, usually in the form of video clips that are digitally altered to show a different face from the original clip. In layman's term, you could essentially swap your face in to Captain America's in an Avengers movie! Despite this, Deepfake has become infamous because it has been wildly used to create indecent clips that may be sexual or political in nature.

How does this affect you? Well, in our world today where we're connected to the internet almost 24/7, we have a quite the digital footprint that's out in the open! In theory, pictures and videos that you've posted online in your social media accounts could be used to make defamatory Deepfakes against you. You could also be victim to Deepfake media that could be easily spread out through the social media as well.

Thankfully, while the technology is powerful, it's still far from perfect. If looked at closely, you could still distinguish what's real from fake. However, the rapid growth in usage of Deepfakes have given rise to the rapid evolution of the technology. In the future, it might be too close to tell what's real anymore. That's where we come in.
